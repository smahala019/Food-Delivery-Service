Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our gift cards

How do I redeem my gift card?
To redeem your gift card, simply enter the unique code at checkout when placing your order. The gift card amount will be automatically deducted from your total.
Can I use multiple gift cards on one order?
Yes, you can use multiple gift cards on a single order. Simply enter each gift card code at checkout, and the amounts will be combined.
What happens if my order total is less than the gift card value?
The remaining balance on your gift card will be saved for future use. You can check your balance anytime using our balance check feature.
Can I get a refund for my gift card?
Gift cards are non-refundable once purchased. However, they are transferable, so you can gift them to someone else if needed.
Do gift cards expire?
Yes, gift cards have an expiration date. Classic and Premium cards are valid for 12 months, Festive cards for 15 months, and Corporate cards for 18 months from the date of purchase.
