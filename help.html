Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar. Fill in your details including name, email, mobile number, and password. Once you submit the form, your account will be created and you can start ordering immediately.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Login" button and then select "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

How do I place an order?

Browse restaurants from the "Restaurants" page, select a restaurant, and add items to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery details, select a payment method, and confirm your order.

Can I customize my order?

Yes! Many menu items offer customization options like spice level, crust type for pizzas, or additional toppings. Simply click on an item and select "Customize" to see available options.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Is it safe to use my credit card on your site?

Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time varies by restaurant and is typically displayed on the restaurant page. Most orders are delivered within 30-45 minutes, but this can be longer during peak hours.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status in real-time through the "Order Tracking" page. You'll receive updates when your order is confirmed, being prepared, out for delivery, and delivered.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before the restaurant starts preparing it. Once the order status changes to "Preparing", cancellation may not be possible.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are offered in case of order issues, delivery problems, or restaurant errors. Please contact our customer support within 24 hours of receiving your order to request a refund.

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account and go to the "My Profile" page. From there, you can update your personal information, delivery addresses, and notification preferences.

Why is the app not working on my phone?

Make sure you have a stable internet connection and are using the latest version of the app. If problems persist, try clearing your browser cache or restarting your device.

My payment failed but I was charged. What should I do?

If your payment failed but you were charged, please contact our customer support immediately with your order details. We'll investigate the issue and process a refund if necessary.
