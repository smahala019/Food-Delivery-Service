How do I place an order?
To place an order, simply browse restaurants, select your favorite dishes, add them to cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your delivery address, choose payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive real-time updates on your order status.
Can I schedule an order for later?
Yes! You can schedule orders up to 7 days in advance. Simply select your items, proceed to checkout, and choose "Schedule Order" instead of "Order Now". Select your preferred date and time, and we'll deliver at your convenience.
How do I cancel my order?
You can cancel your order from the order tracking page if the restaurant hasn't started preparing it yet. Go to "My Orders", select the order, and click "Cancel Order". If the restaurant has already started preparation, cancellation may not be possible.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, digital wallets (PayTM, PhonePe, Google Pay), and cash on delivery. All online payments are secured with industry-standard encryption.
Is it safe to save my card details?
Yes, absolutely. We use PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways and your card details are encrypted and stored securely. We never store your complete card information on our servers, ensuring maximum security.
How do I apply a promo code?
During checkout, you'll see a field labeled "Enter Promo Code". Simply type or paste your code and click "Apply". The discount will be immediately reflected in your order total if the code is valid and applicable.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery time varies based on restaurant preparation time, distance, and traffic conditions. Typically, deliveries take 30-45 minutes. You'll see the estimated delivery time when ordering and can track your delivery in real-time.
What if my delivery is late?
If your order is significantly delayed, you can contact our customer support through the app or website. We may offer compensation in the form of coupons or refunds depending on the situation. You'll also receive real-time updates about any delays.
Can I track my delivery?
Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time. Go to "My Orders" and select your active order. You'll see the restaurant's location, preparation status, and your delivery partner's real-time location on the map.
How do I create an account?
Click on "Sign Up" and enter your name, email, and phone number. You'll receive an OTP to verify your number. Once verified, your account will be created. You can also sign up using Google or Facebook for quicker registration.
How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email or phone number, and we'll send you a reset link or OTP. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
Can I change my delivery address?
Yes, you can add multiple delivery addresses in your profile settings. When placing an order, you can select from saved addresses or add a new one. You can also change the delivery address before confirming your order.
The app is not working properly. What should I do?
Try these troubleshooting steps: 1) Clear app cache, 2) Update to the latest version, 3) Restart your device, 4) Check your internet connection. If issues persist, contact our technical support team with details about the problem.
Why can't I see restaurants in my area?
Make sure location services are enabled and you've granted permission to the app. Check that your delivery address is correct and within our service area. If you still can't see restaurants, try refreshing the app or updating your location.
My payment failed but money was deducted. What now?
Don't worry! If money was deducted but the payment failed, it will be automatically refunded to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. If you don't receive the refund, contact our support team with transaction details.
